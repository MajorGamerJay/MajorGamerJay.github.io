What I use

I usually use Gentoo GNU/Linux for most of my work and daily browsing and Windows 10 for gaming or else I wouldn’t be called majorgamer :^)

  • WM: Openbox
  • Terminal: Konsole
  • File Manager: Ranger
  • Music Player: ncmpcpp (with MPD and http music streams)
  • Browser: Mozilla Firefox
  • Instant Messaging: Irssi and Discord
  • Editor: Neovim
  • Panel/Bar: Tint2
  • Image Editing: GIMP
  • Video Editing: Kdenlive
